Vimeo Speed — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

What data does this extension collect?

None. Vimeo Speed does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

What does the extension store?

The extension stores a single value — your preferred playback speed — using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.sync API. This data is stored locally on your device (and synced across your Chrome profile if you use Chrome Sync). It never leaves your browser.

What permissions does the extension use?

  • storage — To save your preferred speed setting
  • Content scripts on vimeo.com and player.vimeo.com — To inject the speed control script into Vimeo video pages

Third-party services

This extension does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services.

Changes to this policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page.
