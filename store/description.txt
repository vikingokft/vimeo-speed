Vimeo / Bunny Instant Speed - Automatic Video Speed Control

Tired of manually changing the playback speed for every single video? Instant Speed automatically sets your preferred playback speed on all Vimeo and Bunny Stream videos — both on their native sites and embedded players on any website.

FEATURES:
• Set a default playback speed that applies to every video automatically
• Preset speed buttons (1x, 1.25x, 1.5x, 1.75x, 2x) — one click to switch
• Custom speed input for any value between 0.5x and 4x
• Speed overlay appears on the video when you hover — change speed without leaving the player
• Brief speed indicator flashes when a video starts playing
• Works on Vimeo, Bunny Stream, AND their embedded players on any website
• Speed persists across browser sessions — set it once, forget it
• Badge on the extension icon shows your current speed at a glance
• Compatible with native speed menus — use either one

SUPPORTED PLAYERS:
• Vimeo (vimeo.com + embedded players)
• Bunny Stream (mediadelivery.net + embedded players)

PERFECT FOR:
• Online course students watching 1000+ tutorial videos
• Anyone who prefers faster (or slower) video playback
• Teams using Vimeo or Bunny for training or onboarding content

PRIVACY:
This extension only stores your speed preference locally using Chrome's built-in storage. No data is collected, no analytics, no tracking. The extension only runs on supported video player pages.

PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED:
• Storage: To remember your preferred speed across sessions
• Content scripts on Vimeo & Bunny domains: To control video playback speed
