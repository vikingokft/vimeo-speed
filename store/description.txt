Vimeo Speed - Automatic Vimeo Video Speed Control

Tired of manually changing the playback speed for every single Vimeo video? Vimeo Speed automatically sets your preferred playback speed on all Vimeo videos — both on vimeo.com and embedded players on any website.

FEATURES:
• Set a default playback speed that applies to every Vimeo video automatically
• Preset speed buttons (1x, 1.25x, 1.5x, 1.75x, 2x) — one click to switch
• Custom speed input for any value between 0.5x and 4x
• Speed overlay appears on the video when you hover — change speed without leaving the player
• Brief speed indicator flashes when a video starts playing
• Works on vimeo.com AND embedded Vimeo players on any website
• Speed persists across browser sessions — set it once, forget it
• Badge on the extension icon shows your current speed at a glance
• Compatible with Vimeo's native speed menu — use either one

PERFECT FOR:
• Online course students watching 1000+ tutorial videos
• Anyone who prefers faster (or slower) video playback
• Teams using Vimeo for training or onboarding content

PRIVACY:
This extension only stores your speed preference locally using Chrome's built-in storage. No data is collected, no analytics, no tracking. The extension only runs on Vimeo pages.

PERMISSIONS EXPLAINED:
• Storage: To remember your preferred speed across sessions
• Content scripts on vimeo.com: To control video playback speed
